Rules:
Team size: minimum 5 members and maximum 20 member are allowed in a team.

Elimination round:
1.Teams will have to perform to a suitably edited part of the music from their main performance.
2.The music which is used in the elimination round has to be a part of the final round.
3.No marks for costumes and props in prelims.
4.Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
5.Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
6.Teams should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.
Time limit: 4-5 minutes

Final round:
1.Teams present their prepared performances.
2.Time limit: 8-12 minutes ( music piece duration).
3.Costumes and props will be included in the judging criteria this round.
4.The decision of judges shall be final.
