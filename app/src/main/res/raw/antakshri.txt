We've all enjoyed the antakshari on our bus rides, family outings, picnics and honed our impromptu skills at singing those well-known songs but now is the time to showcase the acquired talent and also a chance to win a reward for the same. Even if you've lost touch with the much adorned game, you can always start again and relive those moments of spontaneity and fun.

Rules:

The contest will be conducted in rounds.A preliminary round (written) might be conducted.A team shall comprise of maximum 2 members.Any kind of vulgarity and blasphemy will lead to disqualification.Decision of judges will be final.Registrations can be done online as well as on the spot.

Certificates and Prizes:

Cerificates of participation will be given to all the participating teams.Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.

For registration visit: apratim.ccet.ac.in
