It is all about chemistry, synchronization and a little fun and you are set to tap and dance to the rhythm. So bring your partner and put up a scintillating show like never before and you have prizes waiting for you.


Rules ROUND 1: PRELIMS

1. Track limit: 2-3minutes

2.  No marks for props and costumes

3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.

4. All dance forms are allowed.

5. Participants should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.

6. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.

7. Registrations can be done online and on the spot.

8. The decision of the judges will be final.


Cerificates of participation will be given to all the participating teams. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
