Rules:

Team size: minimum 5 and maximum 30 members limit in a team. However, at any instant, presence of 5 members on the floor is mandatory.


Round 1:

A. Teams will be shortlisted in this round

B. Judging Criteria: choreography, synchronization, presentation, energy and overall impact.

C. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.

D. Teams should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.

E. The decision of the judges will be final.

F. Time limit: 5-8 minutes


Round 2:

A. The teams selected after round 1 will have to battle with each other on the beats played at that time.

B. The team will be judged based on their impromptu skills and presentation.

C. The decision of judges will be final.
